About AACT Integration

This tool validates NCT IDs against the ClinicalTrials.gov API (which mirrors the AACT database). Results are cached in your browser's IndexedDB for faster subsequent lookups.

  • Extracts detailed metadata: sponsor, phase, status, enrollment, locations
  • Supports batch validation of up to 500 NCT IDs
  • Results cached locally for 24 hours
  • Export to CSV, JSON, or RIS format

How the Optimizer Works

The Strategy Optimizer uses historical performance data and machine learning to recommend the best search strategy for your specific use case.

  • Condition Classification: Automatically detects condition category (oncology, cardiology, etc.) for tailored recommendations
  • Bayesian Ranking: Uses Thompson sampling to balance exploitation vs exploration of strategies
  • Wilson Score Confidence: Provides confidence intervals for recall and precision estimates
  • Known Study Bonus: Strategies that can retrieve your known relevant studies get priority
  • Goal Optimization: Weights strategies based on your specific search goal

Search Goal Explanations

Goal Description Best For
Maximum Recall Finds as many relevant studies as possible, even at cost of screening more irrelevant ones Cochrane reviews, HTA reports
Balanced Optimizes F1 score - good trade-off between recall and precision Most systematic reviews
High Precision Minimizes irrelevant results, may miss some relevant studies Rapid reviews, scoping searches
Quick Overview Fast results with reasonable coverage Feasibility checks, protocol development
